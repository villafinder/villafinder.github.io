David Chambat

Managing Director & Founder

David’s job is to make sure that you have the best possible experience when you search, book and stay in one of our villas. Being extremely customer-focused, he insists on talking to clients directly on the phone and personally visiting each of the villas in our collection.

David started the company in 2012 when he realized that booking a villa was still a challenging thing to do. A native of France, he has been living in Asia since 1997 and has worked for Air France-KLM and the hotel website TVtrip.com. He is a graduate from EDHEC and holds an MBA from INSEAD. A keen runner and a passionate yogi, he currently lives in Singapore and has 3 kids.

Laurent Ghirardotti

Chief Technology Officer

Laurent is the wizard that brings the company's dreams to the World Wide Web. A die hard on ensuring the quality, delivery and security of our websites, he hunts down any inelegant syntax in our code. A graduate in Information Technology from Bordeaux University, France, Laurent previously worked for Virgin Mobile, Saint-Gobain (construction materials) and myVideoProfile (on demand sport video) before moving to Singapore in 2015.

Daniel Rouquette

Head of Marketing & Co-founder

Daniel and his marketing team are the product's guardians. They are here to inspect our collection of villas, give you a genuine review of each property and edit great travel guides to help you travel to our destinations. Daniel is a graduate from EDHEC Business School in France, and has a passion for entrepreneurial projects which led him to Singapore in 2010. The only thing he enjoys more than traveling around the world is playing the devil’s advocate in our meetings.
